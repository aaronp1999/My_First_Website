About Pannir

Committed to Health, Purity, and Hydration

Operated under Basavashree Natural Beverages, we combine advanced multi-stage reverse osmosis filtration with state-of-the-art packaging systems to deliver optimal hydration straight to homes, events, and corporations.

Common Questions Answered

Learn more about our strict purification methodology, shipping grids, and customized options.

We source raw spring water from pristine, certified natural underground aquifers. The fluid passes through a rigorous 7-stage purification stack: Dual-Media Filtration, Activated Carbon adsorption, Micron Polishing, High-Pressure Reverse Osmosis (RO) membrane treatment, Ultra-filtration, UV Sterilization, and Ozonation. This ensures absolute micro-biological security while preserving crisp mineral balancing.

Our bottling unit (managed under Basavashree Natural Beverages) is accredited with ISO 22000:2018 (Food Safety Management Systems) certificates. We are fully approved by municipal sanitary panels and conduct dynamic physical, chemical, and biological assays inside our quality control laboratory daily.
